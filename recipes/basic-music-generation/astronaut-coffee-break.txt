Lo-fi jazz waltz at 96 BPM in F major. Brushed drums on a soft snare, walking upright bass, warm Rhodes piano chords, breathy muted trumpet weaving the melody. The faint, gentle clink of a ceramic mug tapping an aluminum bulkhead on every second downbeat. Distant white noise of life-support systems and the slow tick of a pressure gauge. Weightless, contemplative, no urgency. Late-night listening at zero gravity.
